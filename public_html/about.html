About Us

Who We Are

Our PetShop is largest free online pet marketplace, relied upon by more than 5 million people monthly to rehome over 2,700 animals each day. PetShop prioritises responsible practices and works to ensure trust, safety, and convenience for buyers and sellers.

What We Do

For those looking to buy a dog, cat, horse, rabbit, fish or bird , PetShop provides a safe environment in which to find their dream pet. PetShop connects a high volume of serious buyers with responsible breeders.
